About SAPPHIRE OCEAN

Not only provides wonderful stay
but also devotes on maritime education

Welcome to this beautiful island. Clear blue sky, crystal clean ocean, lovely creatures, once you visit here, we're sure you'll fall in love with everthing here.

SAPPHIRE OCEAN is not only a highly praised resort, but committing to leading more people to explore the ocean. Besides, we also promote and implement sustainable diving and marine conservation actions through maritime activities, so that everyone can have better understanding of the current situation and challenges of the underwater ecosystem. while having the good memories, everyone is one of maritime members.
