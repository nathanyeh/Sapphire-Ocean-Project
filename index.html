About SAPPHIRE OCEAN

This is how the story begins and our responsibility

The pandemic gathers us in this beautiful island. Clear ocean, lovely creatures, and friendly residents, we enjoy every single moment here. After going back to mainland Taiwan, we decide to come back here as we miss everthing here, and it is where we love.
